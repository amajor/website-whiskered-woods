Ordering
When do pre-orders open and close?

Pre-orders open Saturday mornings and close Tuesday nights at 9pm. This gives us enough time to source supplies, prep, and bake so everything arrives fresh for your Friday pickup — ready for family dinner or a weekend gathering.

Can I order same-day or after the window closes?

Unfortunately no. We're a small family microbakery with dedicated baking days built around our schedule, and we bake to order so nothing goes to waste. Ordering ahead is the only way to guarantee your spot!

Pickup
When and where is pickup?

Porch pickup is every Friday from 3–6pm in Zeeland. The exact address is included in each Hotplate pre-order and will also be texted to you when your order is ready.

We're just around the corner from Captain Sundae and right down the street from Zeeland Christian School — an easy stop during school pickup, on the way home from work, or during a late afternoon walk.

What if I can't make pickup?

No problem — send a friend, or message us through the Hotplate app and we'll do our best to work something out. We can't always accommodate requests outside our scheduled pickup window, but we're happy to try if you need us to leave your order outside or have someone else swing by.

Do you deliver?

We're open to delivering within biking distance of our home for a small additional fee. Send us a message if that's something you'd like to work out!

Are your products made in a licensed kitchen?

All of our baked goods are made following the Michigan Cottage Food Law, which permits home-kitchen food businesses to operate without a commercial kitchen license. Every product is labeled with the required statement and a full ingredient list.

You can learn more at michigan.gov/mdard.

Do you accommodate allergies?
Our home kitchen handles wheat, eggs, dairy, and tree nuts. We are not able to guarantee an allergen-free environment.

That said, we label everything with a full ingredient list and are happy to help you figure out what might work for your situation — just send us a message before ordering.

Storage & Reheating
How should I store sourdough focaccia and how long does it last?

Sourdough focaccia keeps well at room temperature for 2–3 days wrapped in a clean towel or loosely covered. For longer storage, slice it first and freeze in an airtight bag — it'll keep for up to 3 months.

Can I freeze it, and how do I reheat it?

Yes! Slice before freezing and store in an airtight bag for up to 3 months. To reheat:

  • Oven (best): 375°F for 8–10 minutes from room temp, or 12–15 minutes from frozen
  • Air fryer: 350°F for 5–7 minutes — comes back wonderfully crisp
  • Microwave: Works in a pinch but softens the crust — not recommended if you have another option
It comes out tasting like fresh homemade bread. You can absolutely take the credit. Find more tips and serving ideas on our Instagram.
About Us
Who makes the food?

Alison is the head baker, but this is genuinely a family operation. Our kids have been growing their kitchen skills for years — including nearly three years living and cooking aboard our sailboat. Everyone has a hand in what we make here.

What is Bob?

Bob is our sourdough starter — the living culture of wild yeast and bacteria that gives our focaccia its long fermentation and distinctive flavor. He's been part of the family for a while now and takes his job very seriously. Every loaf starts with Bob.

Where are you located?

We're in Zeeland, Michigan — West Michigan, in the Holland/Zeeland area. The exact pickup address is shared with each Hotplate order.

How do I get in touch?

The easiest way to reach us is through the messaging feature in the Hotplate app if you have an order. You can also email us at hello@whiskeredwoods.com and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.

Policies
What is your refund and cancellation policy?

We bake to order, so cancellations impact us more than a traditional bakery. Full refunds (minus payment processing fees) are available for cancellations 24+ hours before pickup. Within 24 hours, we're unable to refund as your order is already in progress.

For larger orders (6+ items), please cancel 48+ hours ahead. Something came up? Reach out — we're a real family and we'll always try to work with you.

Is this a licensed business?

Yes. We operate as Whiskered Woods, LLC under the Michigan Cottage Food Law, which permits home-based food businesses to sell directly to customers. Every product is properly labeled in compliance with state requirements.
